SOME FACTS

Besiktas (BJK in short) was founded in 1903 in Istanbul, about a decade before WWI and about two decades before Turkey (Republic of Turkey) was founded in 1923 upon the ruins of the Ottoman Empire.

Besiktas is also home to the affluent suburb of contemporary Istanbul. It is by the Bosphorus that connects the Asian side of the city to the European side. Besiktas is on the European side of Istanbul.

The team's mascot is the black eagle. Besiktas Football Club is also referred to as the Black Eagles ( "Kara Kartallar" in Turkish )
